Flood Preparedness

• Prepare an emergency kit (Food, clear water, medicine, flashlights).

• Secure important identification and legal documents in waterproof bags.

• Identify high-ground safe locations and rapid evacuation routes.

• Evacuate immediately if local rescue teams or authorities advise it.

• Avoid walking or driving through moving water currents.

• Turn off main electrical switches and gas valves to prevent hazards.

• Do not enter damaged structures until they are cleared by safety teams.

• Drink boiled or clean bottled water to prevent waterborne diseases.

• Clean and disinfect everything that got wet during the flood surge.
