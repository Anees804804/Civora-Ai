CIVORA AI - PAKISTAN EMERGENCY FIRST AID GUIDELINES

1. HEART ATTACK (DIL KA DAURA):
- Mareez ko seedha bitha dein aur aaram se saans lene dein.
- Agar hosh mein ho to foran Aspirin (Disprin) ki goli chabane ko dein.
- Foran Rescue 1122 par call karein.

2. BLEEDING (KHOON BEHNA):
- Zakham par saaf kapra rakh kar zor se dabayein (direct pressure).
- Agar khoon na ruke, to zakham wali jagah ko dil ke level se upar utha dein.
- Ganda kapra ya mitti zakham par na lagayein.

3. BURNS (JAL JANA):
- Jali hui jagah par foran 10-15 minute thanda pani dalein.
- Ice (barf) direct zakham par na lagayein kyun ke yeh tissues ko damage karti hai.
- Toothpaste ya ghee lagane se parhez karein. Saaf patti se dhanp dein.

4. HEATSTROKE (LOO LAGNA):
- Mareez ko foran saye dar ya thandey kamre mein shift karein.
- Geela kapra jism par rakhein taake temperature kam ho.
- Agar hosh mein ho to thanda pani ya ORS dein.

5. CHOKING (GALA GHUTNA / SAANS RUKNA):
- Mareez ki peeth par zor se 5 martba hath marein (Back slaps).
- Agar farq na pare to pet ko peche se pakad kar upar ki taraf khinchein (Heimlich Maneuver).

EMERGENCY HELPLINES IN PAKISTAN:
- Rescue: 1122
- Edhi Ambulance: 115
- Police: 15
